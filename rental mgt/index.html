About Cwezi Apartments

The name “Cwezi” comes from the ‘Abacawezi” a mythical, magical tribe that was once the ruling dynasty of the great Kingdom of Bunyoro Kitara. Our logo, featuring two African style huts, symbolizes the spirit of warm hospitality that we extend to all our guests and residents. We strive to deliver the right mix of luxury, comfort, functionality and value for money at all of our three locations. Our location is carefully chosen to ensure easy access to business, shopping, leisure and educational facilities while providing the quiet and homely environment of good residential neighborhoods. Our two and 2 bedroom units is just the thing to cater to the needs of a typical expatriate family. We are located in Naguru and continue to develop new locations and to expand our product mix. We look forward to receiving you!
